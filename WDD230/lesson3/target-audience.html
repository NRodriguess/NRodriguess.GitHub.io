Target Audience


Two Personas


Persona 1: A worker


She is a 32-year-old woman who works for a hair and skin care products company. Most of her job is to go from door to door ordering your products. Ana does this part of the work waking, so every day before leaving her house, she checks the Weather Site to see if it is going to rain.


Persona 2 A professional ski athlete


He is a professional ski athlete. He trains practically every day of the year. He loves this sport and for this reason, he is always looking for different places with snow to ski and train. For a week, He looks at a weather site to see places where there is a lot of snow so he can ski. He likes to explore new places and that is why the site is so important to him.
